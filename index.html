WHAT IS DECODE

◈
The Concept
Decode '26 is a closed-door evening for 50 decision-makers who evaluate, buy, and deploy AI Sales Automation tools across Bengaluru's B2B companies. Not a conference. Not a webinar. Not a vendor event.

The name says it all — we're here to decode what's real about AI Sales Automation from the hype, the noise, and the LinkedIn hot takes. Think: a private venue revealed closer to the date, good energy, a curated playlist, and 50 of the city's sharpest sales technology decision-makers having the conversations that don't happen on stage.
⚡
The Theme
AI Sales Automation in 2026 — automated outreach, agentic SDRs, intelligent pipeline management, and AI-led deal intelligence. What's actually working, what's been overhyped, and what's coming next. No theory. Just what practitioners are actually seeing.
🔒
The Code
Chatham House Rule. Nothing leaves the room with attribution. No recording. No screenshots. No live-tweeting. What's decoded here, stays decoded here. That's the deal.
