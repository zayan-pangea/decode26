WHAT IS DECODE

◈
The Concept
Decode '26 is a closed-door evening for 50 sales professionals and the leaders behind them. People who live inside a sales stack every single day, open their CRM on Monday morning, run sequences, chase pipeline, and are now figuring out where AI genuinely fits into all of that.

We are here to decode one thing: which AI tools are actually making sales professionals better at their jobs, and which ones are just making the deck look good.
⚡
The Theme
The tools sales pros actually live in. CRMs, sequencing tools, AI dialers, intent data platforms, forecasting software.

What is worth paying for, what is getting replaced, and what no one talks about on demo calls.
🚪
The Code
Leave the Script at the Door — No positioning. No carefully worded answers. No version of yourself that you would put on a LinkedIn post.

Just an honest conversation about what is working, what is not, and what the sales tools of 2026 actually feel like from the inside.

THE DECODES

Three questions on AI Sales Automation. No right answers. Just what you actually believe.

01
Gartner — 75% of B2B sales orgs will use AI-guided selling by 2025. Yet the average rep still spends less than 30% of their time actually selling. Which tool in your stack is solving that — and which one is adding to the admin?
02
McKinsey — Companies using AI in sales have seen revenue uplifts of up to 15% and cost reductions of up to 20%.
What does that number look like in your org — and what did it actually take to get there?
03
Forrester — 68% of buyers prefer not to interact with a sales rep during the early stages of the buying process. Is your sales stack built for a world where the first five touchpoints happen? before a human ever picks up the phone?
